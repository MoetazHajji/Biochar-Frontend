Forgot Password

If an email user forgets their password, they can easily reset it by clicking the "send" link on the login page. They will then be prompted to enter their email address, and aconfirmation email will be sent to that address. This email will contain a link to a page where the user can enter a new password. It's important to remember to choose a strong, unique password that can't be easily guessed or hacked.

Email is required
