The Chief Service in our system has the authority to modify the roles of the users. The list of users in our system includes patients, doctors, receptionists, biologists, interns, and other members of the healthcare team. With the Chief Service's permission, a user's role can be changed to better reflect their position or responsibilities within the system. In addition, the Chief Service can modify the enable status of users. This is important because some users may need to be temporarily disabled due to leave or other reasons, and the Chief Service can easily manage this with just a few clicks. With these capabilities, the Chief Service has full control over the users in our system and can make changes as needed to ensure smooth operation of the healthcare service.
