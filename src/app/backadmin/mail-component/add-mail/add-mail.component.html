Mail

In our system, we have a built-in feature that allows the sending of emails to patients and employees. When sending an email, the user can choose the recipient(s) and enter a subject for the email. The subject should be brief and give an idea of what the email is about. The user can also enter the text content of the email, which should be informative and concise. It's important to ensure that the content is well-written and clearly conveys the message to avoid confusion or misunderstandings. Once the email has been composed, the user can click the send button and the email will be sent to the intended recipients. Our system also allows for tracking the status of sent emails to ensure that they have been successfully delivered to the recipients.
